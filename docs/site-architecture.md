# Site Architecture

This file defines the public-facing narrative structure for `japanesejesus.com`.

The URLs stay stable for continuity and discoverability. The framing changes.

## Core Principle

Japanese Jesus is a myth engine, not a travel brochure and not a religious explainer.

The public site should move in this order:

1. Establish the portal myth.
2. Explain the canon through the Four Epochs.
3. Ground that canon in Shingo as a real frontier landscape.
4. Offer the journey north as a deliberate act.
5. Present products as relics from the system.

## Route Roles

### `/`

The landing page is the portal entry.

- Lead with the Shingo conduit and the threshold myth.
- State the canon in compressed form.
- Route people into the Four Epochs, the Conduit, the journey north, and the relic catalog.
- Keep the tone atmospheric, severe, and legible.

### `/canon`

This route is the canonical backbone.

- Frame the lore explicitly as the Four Epochs.
- Keep the order stable:
  1. The Wandering Spirit
  2. Incarnation and Burden
  3. The Long Walk East
  4. The Conduit Unseals
- Isukiri remains a structural witness inside the second epoch, not a co-protagonist.
- The interactive trial at `/canon/game` is a canon-aligned subroute and should stay framed as a playable index of these four epochs.

### `/conduit`

This route grounds the myth in geography.

- Present Shingo as the primary node.
- Use field-note language, coordinates, and seasonal atmosphere.
- Emphasize the conduit, the seam, and the frontier landscape over tomb-tourism framing.

### `/journey`

This route is the operational journey page.

- Treat travel as a threshold approach, not a conventional itinerary.
- Keep the route useful, but let the mood remain deliberate and mythic.
- The page should feel like a journey briefing: how to get there, where to stay, and how the approach changes the mood.

### `/relics`

This route is the relic catalog.

- Products should read as artifacts, signals, field gear, and wall pieces.
- Avoid generic merch language.
- The public catalog should feel like recovered proof from the conduit system.

### `/signal`

This route is the clean signal intake page.

- Centralize transmission signup and signal language here.
- Keep the tone sparse and direct.
- This page should feel like entering a live channel, not joining a newsletter.

## Legacy Routes

The previous public URLs remain as legacy aliases and should redirect:

- `/the-legend` -> `/canon`
- `/shingo-today` -> `/conduit`
- `/get-there` -> `/journey`
- `/walk` -> `/journey`
- `/objects` -> `/relics`

## Shared Copy Rules

- Prefer: threshold, conduit, seam, signal, field, frontier, static, north, memory, residue.
- Avoid: sermons, doctrine, salvation, pilgrimage in a devotional sense, tourist attraction framing.
- Keep the mythology legible without over-explaining it.
- Keep the site readable to a first-time visitor while still feeling like a living canon.
